The location of each site was either calculated based on the legal description of the facility or obtained from visual inspection of either the 2002 CIR DOQs or the NAIP aerial photography from 2004 or after. Process_Step:
Process_Description:
Points are initially located to the center of the section, or if available, two quarter sections. Then information from the IDNR AFO database was used to match up the location with buildings identified from the 2002 CIR DOQs or NAIP aerial photography. The legal description or address was mainly used to obtain the general location and then the aerial photo was used to place the point on a building at each site, if it appeared on the aerial photo.
